Privacy Policy - upNine

Last updated: April 25, 2025

upNine ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you use the app:

Personal Information
  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • Location data (if permissions are granted)
  • Media you upload (photos, videos, etc.)
Device Information
  • Device model, OS version, unique identifiers
  • Usage statistics and crash logs
Collected via Firebase:
  • Push notification tokens (via FCM)
  • Analytics (via Firebase Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver content and features
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send notifications (if opted in)
  • Analyze usage trends (anonymously)

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share limited data with:

  • Firebase (for backend services)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Your Privacy Choices

  • You can revoke permissions (location, camera, microphone) via your device settings.
  • You can delete your account by contacting us at apchallengeus@gmail.com

5. Security

We use encryption and secure storage techniques to protect your information.
